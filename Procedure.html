How to Change Your Profile Picture in Facebook Without Notifying Others?

To change your profile pic in Facebook discreetly, follow the procedure provided below

  1. Log in to your Facebook account using your login credentials.

  2. Navigate to your Profile page.

  3. Hover your mouse over your current profile picture.

  4. Click on Update profile picture option.

  5. Browse the location in which you have saved your pictures and click Upload photo to upload your new picture.

An update on your new profile picture is automatically sent to you and your friends you are connected with.

  1. Click the Globe symbol visible next to the date/time option in the update you received.

    2. A drop down menu with the options Public, Friends, Friends except, Specific friends, and Only me gets displayed.

    1. Click the Only me option to stop notifying others about new profile picture.

      2. Congratulations! You have successfully uploaded your profile picture without notifying anyone else.
